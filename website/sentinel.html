How this works

Every day, an AI-agent session fetches the last 72 hours of press releases and news items from the ECB, US Federal Reserve, SEC, Bank of England, FCA and EBA. The agent reads each item, assigns a 1–5 relevance score from a frontier-finance risk perspective, categorises it, and writes a one-line summary plus a one-line "why it matters" for a CRO at a fintech or crypto fund. The result is the ranked list above.

This same agent stack powers the Risk AI engagements EON delivers to clients — adapted to the specific regulators, jurisdictions and risk surfaces of each firm. If you’d like a private version of this running against your own risk universe, book a discovery call.

Hands-On Implementation Support

For banks that need expert guidance building, fixing, or validating their risk identification process.

Engagement Types

Three ways to work together, depending on where you are and what you need.

Gap Assessment

Independent review of your current risk identification process against the methodology and 16 regulatory frameworks. Identifies weaknesses before the regulator does.

Deliverable: Gap report with prioritised remediation plan
Typical duration: 5 days
Implementation

Design and build the full process from scratch — governance framework, risk taxonomy, templates, workshop facilitation, capital planning integration. Includes facilitation of the first full identification cycle.

Deliverable: Working process + first cycle facilitated
Typical duration: 20–40 days
Audit-Readiness Review

Stress-test your existing process against what supervisors actually look for in ICAAP reviews, SREP, and CCAR. Includes a mock regulatory Q&A session.

Deliverable: Readiness report + mock Q&A
Typical duration: 3–5 days
